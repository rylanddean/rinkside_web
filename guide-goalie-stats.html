Roster

Finding Your Goalie

Step 1

Open the Roster and scroll to the Goalies section

Tap the Roster tab at the bottom of the screen. Skaters are listed first — keep scrolling until you reach the Goalies section. Tap any goalie's row to open their profile.

🥅 Goalies are automatically grouped separately from skaters. You don't need to filter — Rinkside organises the roster for you.
2

Profile Overview

Details Tab

Step 1

Review the goalie's profile details

The profile opens on the Details tab, showing the goalie's jersey number, catching hand, position, and the team they're rostered on.

✏️ Tap Edit in the top-right corner to update any profile field — jersey number, catches, or team — without leaving the screen.
3

Season Numbers

Stats & Report Card

Step 1

Tap the Stats tab to see season totals

Tap the Stats tab to view the goalie stats card. All six key metrics are displayed at a glance, with games played shown below the divider.

W — Wins L — Losses T — Ties GA — Goals Against GAA — Goals Against Avg SO — Shutouts
📊 GAA is calculated automatically from total goals allowed and minutes played across all logged games — no manual entry needed.
Step 2

Tap the Report Card to rate goalie skills

Scroll down past the stats card to find the Report Card. Tap it to open the rating sheet, then assign a rating to each goalie-specific skill area.

Needs Work On Track Superstar
💡 Goalie Report Cards cover skills like Positioning, Puck Tracking, Rebound Control, and Compete Level — tailored specifically for net-minders.
Step 3

Review ratings inline on the Stats tab

Once rated, each skill shows its current status directly on the Stats tab — so you always have a full picture of stats and development in one place. Tap the Report Card at any time to update a rating.

🔒 Ratings are private to you as the coach and feed directly into the Coaching tab's AI-generated insights.
4

AI Insights

Coaching Tab

Step 1

Tap the Coaching tab for AI-generated insights

The Coaching tab uses on-device Apple Intelligence to surface personalised insights based on your goalie's season stats and Report Card ratings. Each summary includes a season overview, a top strength, and a development focus to guide your next practice conversation.

🤖 Apple Intelligence runs entirely on-device — no data leaves your phone. Insights are available on iPhone 15 Pro and later and update as you log more games and refresh ratings.
Step 2

Add your own coaching notes

Below the Apple Intelligence section you'll find a free-text Coaching Notes field. Use it to capture observations from practice or after a tough game — anything worth remembering before your next chat with the goalie or their parents.

📝 Notes are saved locally on your device and are always available offline — even without a network connection at the rink.
