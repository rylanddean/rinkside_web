Rinkside Beta Is Live — AI-Powered Coaching Tools for Minor Hockey

After a first season behind the bench that exposed a real gap in what coaching tools could offer, Rinkside is now available on TestFlight. Here's everything packed into the beta — and why it exists at all.

Rinkside is a new iOS app built from the ground up for minor hockey coaches. It's available today on TestFlight, with a full App Store release coming soon.

The app covers the full arc of the coaching experience: building and managing a roster, tracking player development throughout the season, running lines during a game, pulling in your schedule automatically, and using on-device AI to help make better decisions — all without a subscription, and without sending your data anywhere.

"I've been around minor hockey my whole life — as a player, as a goalie, then as a hockey parent cheering from the stands, and now in my first year behind the bench as a coach. That first season showed me pretty quickly that there was nothing out there that could help a coaching staff build effective lines or track a player's development over the course of a season. A big thank you to Ross Smith for pushing me to actually build something about it. What started as a simple line builder grew into something much bigger."
— Rylan Dean, Creator of Rinkside

That's the honest origin of this app: one first-year coach, one clear problem, and a decision to build the solution. Here's what made it into the beta.

What's Inside the Beta

🤖

Apple Intelligence Coaching Insights Full Unlock

Rinkside taps into Apple Intelligence to generate personalized coaching insights for each player on your roster — entirely on-device, with no data sent to a server. Fill out a player's report card with ratings across skating, shooting, defense, and more, add your own observations in the notes field, and Rinkside generates a coaching summary grounded in exactly what you've told it. It reads less like an AI output and more like a second opinion from someone who's watched every practice. Insights automatically regenerate when you update a player's card, so they stay current as the season progresses.

🏒

AI Lines Builder Full Unlock

Stop guessing and start building. The AI Lines Builder uses a custom scoring engine that evaluates your players' report card ratings and automatically suggests line combinations across all three forward lines and two defensive pairings. Coaches can review the strategy sheet, preview the full suggested lineup, and adjust from there. It handles even strength, power play, and penalty kill — giving you a structured starting point that reflects what you actually know about your players, not a random shuffle.

🎮

Game Day Command Center

Everything you need between whistles, in one place. The Game Day tab puts your line cards front and center, lets you tap to mark the active line currently on ice, and fires a configurable shift buzzer when it's time for a change. Track shots on goal and saves in real time, follow the live score, and scratch players who aren't dressed — all without ever leaving the screen. It's built to work in a cold arena with gloves on.

📅

Schedule & League Integration

Pull in your full season schedule automatically. Rinkside supports iCalendar import so you can sync directly from your league's scheduling system, and includes a native GameSheet integration that lets you search for your league, select your division, and import your schedule in a few taps. Game results are parsed automatically — including scores, ties, and practices — and your team record updates as the season goes. Tournament schedules are tracked separately, with their own wins, losses, and ties. A team record widget keeps the standings summary visible at the top of your schedule view all season long.

🃏

Hockey Card Export Full Unlock

Generate a hockey card-style graphic for any player on your roster and share it directly from the app. It's a small thing that goes a long way — coaches use it for end-of-season ceremonies, team social posts, or just to give a kid something to be proud of.

Everything Else

Beyond the five headline features, the beta ships with a lot more that makes the day-to-day of coaching easier:

Roster & Player Profiles — Build your full team roster with player details, photos, positions, and jersey numbers. Search and filter in seconds. Player profiles include a tabbed view for details, season stats, and coaching notes.

Season Stats — Track skater and goalie stats throughout the season from the roster tab. Goals, assists, points, GAA, save percentage — the numbers that matter to a minor hockey coach.

Point Streak Badges — Active point streaks are surfaced automatically on the roster list and player profile, along with a personal best badge so you can see when a player is in the form of their season.

Power Play & Penalty Kill Builders — Beyond even strength, Rinkside includes dedicated unit builders for special teams. Build your first and second PP unit and your PK pairings separately from your five-on-five lines.

AirDrop Roster Transfer — Export your full roster as a .rinksideroster file and AirDrop it to another coach's device. Useful for assistant coaches, co-coaches, or season-to-season backups.

Report Card Notifications — Rinkside will send a local notification when a player's report card hasn't been updated in a while, so player development tracking doesn't fall through the cracks mid-season.

AI-Generated Avatars — Using Apple's Image Playground, Rinkside can generate a hockey-themed avatar for any player — a fun addition to the profile that kids genuinely get a kick out of.

Multi-Team Support — Create, switch between, and manage multiple teams from a single app. Coaches who work with more than one team don't need a workaround.
